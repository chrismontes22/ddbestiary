The most dangerous moves to watch out for here are:

  • Orthodemolishers’ Self-Detonate
  • Lesser Orthos Dragons’ Swinge
  • Orthoknights’ Electromagnetism

Pomanders of affluence, flight, and alteration do not drop on floor 29.

Potsherds do not drop from bronze chests on floors 21-23.

AbilityPotencyEffect
Creature of Darkness n/a spawns adds at the front of the arena that move toward the back, leaving two open spaces
Dark Wyrmtail 250 wide line AoE down the center of the arena with late telegraph - get OUT. Spawns adds which move from the center to the sides
Dark Wyrmwing 250 wide line AoEs down both sides of the arena with late telegraph - get IN. Spawns adds which move from each side to the other
Dark Megaflare 250 four sets of telegraphed circle AoEs covering the arena, leaving random spaces open each time; adds spawn from three of the AoEs after each set, moving toward a random player
Whei Morn 250 telegraphed circle AoE, with four additional hits chasing the targeted player - keep moving! Spawns an add moving toward a random player on each hit
Attack (Dark Wanderer adds) 120 triggered whenever you touch an add

Notes:

  • All damage other than the boss's auto-attack inflicts vulnerability up (20% per stack, 1m)
  • Initial actions:
    • Creature of Darkness
    • Dark Wyrmtail OR Dark Wyrmwing
    • Dark Megaflare
    • Whei Morn
    • Dark Wyrmwing OR Dark Wyrmtail
  • Rotation (2m12s per cycle):
    • Creature of Darkness
    • Dark Wyrmtail OR Dark Wyrmwing
    • Whei Morn
    • Dark Wyrmwing OR Dark Wyrmtail
    • Dark Megaflare
    • Whei Morn
    • Dark Wyrmtail OR Dark Wyrmwing
  • The first two Dark Wyrm actions will be either tail followed by wing or wing followed by tail. Likewise, the 3 tail/wing actions in a single rotation will always be either tail-wing-tail or wing-tail-wing
  • A Doga demiclone will defeat the boss in about 7m45s with no player assistance
