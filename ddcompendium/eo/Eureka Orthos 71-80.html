EO 71-80

< Prev
Next >

Floors 71-73 are known to have a lot of landmines. Tanks especially can use pomander of sight on these floors to take advantage of this.

Boss: Proto-Kaliya

AbilityPotencyEffect
Resonance 260 conal AoE tankbuster
Barofield n/a creates damage zones in center (instant death) and outer ring (unique DoT potency 200) of arena
Nanospore Jet n/a inflicts positive or negative charge to each player and spawns four adds outside the arena with positive or negative charge markers; also re-creates the instant death zone in the center of the arena
Auto-cannons (adds) 150 telegraphed line AoEs across the arena, forming an 'X'; used periodically - see rotation below
Centralized Nerve Gas 700 wide conal AoE to the front; the boss turns to face the target before using this
Leftward Nerve Gas 700 180 degree AoE to the boss's left and behind
Rightward Nerve Gas 700 180 degree AoE to the boss's right and behind
Nerve Gas Ring 700 donut AoE with late telegraph - only a narrow ring around the center is safe; instant death zone in center disappears right after this

Notes:

  • Rotation (61s per cycle):
    • Resonance
    • Barofield (only used on first rotation)
    • Nanospore Jet
    • Nerve Gas (Centralized, Leftward, or Rightward)
    • Nerve Gas (Centralized, Leftward, or Rightward)
      • Auto-cannons fire during this cast
    • Nerve Gas (Centralized, Leftward, or Rightward)
      • Auto-cannons fire during this cast
    • Magnet tether appears
    • Nerve Gas (Centralized, Leftward, or Rightward)
      • Auto-cannons fire during this cast
    • Nerve Gas Ring
      • Magnet attract/repel happens just before this cast finishes; there is a quick moment to adjust when the telegraph appears
      • Auto-cannons fire at the same time
  • Getting hit by Auto-cannons or any Nerve Gas attack inflicts vulnerability up (10% per stack, 30s)
  • North and south adds are always negative. West and east adds are always positive
  • If you are solo, you will always be bound to the north add, which spawns behind the boss
  • Use steel if you're not confident, so you can easily survive a hit from any of the Nerve Gas abilities
  • A Doga demiclone will defeat the boss in about 10m45s with no player assistance
