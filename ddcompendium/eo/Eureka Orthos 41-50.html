AbilityPotencyEffect
The Dragon's Voice 900 untelegraphed donut AoE - get IN. Only used as part of the Songs and Thunderous Cold / Cold Thunder combos (never used on its own)
The Ram's Voice 900 untelegraphed pointblank AoE - get OUT. Only used as part of the Songs and Thunderous Cold / Cold Thunder combos (never used on its own)
Songs of Ice and Thunder n/a Ram's Voice followed by Dragon's Voice - get out, then in
Songs of Thunder and Ice n/a Dragon's Voice followed by Ram's Voice - get in, then out
Leftbreathed Thunder 900 untelegraphed 180 degree AoE to the boss's left
Rightbreathed Cold 900 untelegraphed 180 degree AoE to the boss's right
Thunderous Cold 110 telegraphed charge AoE with tether, followed by Dragon's Voice, followed by Ram's Voice - get far to reduce charge damage, then get in, then out
Cold Thunder 110 telegraphed charge AoE with tether, followed by Ram's Voice, followed by Dragon's Voice - get far to reduce charge damage, then get out, then in
Cacophony n/a spawns an orb that tethers and chases each party member for a short time before stopping and exploding; runs to the middle to cast this
Chaotic Chorus (orb add) 600 pointblank thunder AoE; used when the orb gets within range of its target or 9 seconds after it starts moving

Notes:

  • Initial actions:
    • Songs of Ice and Thunder OR Thunder and Ice
    • Leftbreathed Thunder OR Rightbreathed Cold
    • Thunderous Cold OR Cold Thunder
    • Leftbreathed Thunder OR Rightbreathed Cold
  • Rotation (1m18s per cycle):
    • Cacophony
    • Leftbreathed Thunder OR Rightbreathed Cold
    • Thunderous Cold OR Cold Thunder
    • Leftbreathed Thunder OR Rightbreathed Cold
    • Songs of Ice and Thunder OR Thunder and Ice
  • Elements not important for Left/Rightbreathed. For other abilities, pay attention to the order of elements in the ability name - ice/cold is pointblank and thunder is donut
  • All ice/thunder AoEs deal 900 potency magic damage (~50k to a DPS/healer) and inflict vulnerability up (20% per stack, 1m) along with deep freeze (magic DoT potency 150, 6s) or paralysis (15s), uncurable with Esuna
  • Tanks can survive Ram's + Dragon's Voice combo with steel. DPS and healers may die to deep freeze DoT or a following auto-attack
  • Charge tether is potency 2700 when too close, which will one-shot DPS/healers through steel
  • If you are solo, your Cacaphony orb will always spawn west of center
  • A Doga demiclone will defeat the boss in about 4m45s with no player assistance
