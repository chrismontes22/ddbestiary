The set everyone spams for exp. Elder magicite cannot be found in this set. Pomanders of affluence, flight, and alteration do not drop on floor 29.

AbilityPotencyEffect
Lightning Strike 350 telegraphed line AoE
Cloud Call n/a summons Raiuns (untargetable lightning cloud adds)
Shiko 100-1000 proximity AoE; knocks players into the air. Potency scales from 1000 on top of the marker to 100 at ~3y distance (1 boss radius) from the marker edge, so as long as you move a little bit away, damage is minimal
Supercell 300 180 degree AoE (covers the entire room in front of the boss)
Lightning Bolt (cloud AoE) 130 telegraphed circle AoE; inflicts vulnerability up (20% per stack, 1m). Vulnerability up is applied even if the damage is fully shielded

Notes:

  • Opening sequence:
    • Lightning Strike
    • Cloud Call
    • Shiko
      • Make sure you're far from the boss and stand under a cloud. You will break the cloud, creating a safe spot, as the other clouds will all do AoEs a moment later - stay in the safe spot until after this
    • Lightning Strike (wait until cloud AoEs before dodging)
  • Rotation after opening sequence:
    • Lightning Strike
    • Lightning Strike
    • Supercell
    • Cloud Call
    • Shiko
    • Supercell (wait until cloud AoEs before dodging)
