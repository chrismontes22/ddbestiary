Notes:

  • Rotation:
    • Firewalker
    • First set of 4 Accursed Canes appears
    • Second set of 4 Accursed Canes appears

    • Second set of canes does donut AoEs
    • Topple - At the same time, the first group of canes does line AoEs at random players

    • Canes disappear and a new group of 8 appears
    • Fire II
    • All 8 canes do donut AoEs. The centre is always safe from these, and there are safe spots to the outside of each cane
    • Ancient Flare
    • All 8 canes do line AoEs at random players. These are easiest to dodge if you bait them toward the walls
  • TLDR: Lots of AoEs to dodge
