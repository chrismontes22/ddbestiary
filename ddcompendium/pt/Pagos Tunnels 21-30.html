A few things to watch out for:

  • Forgiven Insult’s Holy, which stuns you just like the WHM version
  • Traverse Clionid’s Parasitism, a heavy damage attack used at low health
  • Forgiven Venery, which will chase players to cast its out-of-combat Flare
  • Forgiven Intolerance’s Swinge, a late-telegraph pointblank AoE (unlike the conal seen in EO 21+)

Pomanders of affluence, flight, and alteration do not drop on floor 29.

Potsherds do not drop from bronze chests on floors 21-23.

Completing this set awards a potsherd and a +1/+1 aetherpool upgrade.

Boss: Forgiven Treachery

AbilityPotencyEffect
Liturgy of Light n/a spawns arena damage zones (see rotation)
Brutal Halo 300 ring AoEs spawned by Liturgy of Light
Bounds of Indulgence 300 spinning circle AoEs spawned by Liturgy of Light
Grip of Salvation 300 half-room AoE with late telegraph on the side of the flashing arm; inflicts vulnerability up (20% per stack, 1m)
Salvation's Reach 300 225-degree AoE with late telegraph covering the opposite side of the arena from Grip of Salvation plus a frontal cone (get behind the glowing arm); knocks players upward and inflicts vulnerability up (20% per stack, 1m)
Divine Favor 300 8 circle AoEs chasing the targeted player - keep moving! Inflicts vulnerability up (20% per stack, 1m)

Notes:

  • Initial actions:
    • Liturgy of Light (5 rings)
      • The rings descend from above, each causing a Brutal Halo AoE when they land
    • Grip of Salvation (hits at the same time as the 5th ring)
    • Salvation's Reach
    • Liturgy of Light (spinning circles)
      • Generates a pair of spinning circle zones (either far + middle or far + near) on each cardinal, which then slowly rotate around the arena. Standing in one triggers Bounds of Indulgence once per second
    • Grip of Salvation
    • Salvation's Reach
    • Grip of Salvation
    • Salvation's Reach
  • Rotation after opening (57s per cycle):
    • Divine Favor
    • Liturgy of Light (6 rings + spinning circles)
    • Grip of Salvation (hits with the 4th ring)
    • Salvation's Reach (hits with the 6th ring)
  • The Liturgy of Light AoEs don't apply vulnerability up, so if you get trapped, it's better to run through one of those than get hit by Grip of Salvation or Salvation's Reach
  • Feo Ul will defeat the boss in about 7m using barkbalm incense with no player assistance
