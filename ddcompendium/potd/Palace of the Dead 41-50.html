PotD 41-50

< Prev
Next >

Mimics on this set and beyond are immune to stun, and found in gold chests. There are lots of wall traps to watch out for.

Pomanders of alteration, resolution, and raising do not drop at all on this set. Affluence and flight do not drop on floor 49.

Potsherds do not drop from bronze chests on floors 41-43.

Beating this set unlocks the option to start new files at floor 51.

Boss: Edda Blackbosom

AbilityPotencyEffect
Dark Harvest 120 cleave
In Health 400 either a huge donut AoE or a large pointblank AoE; pierces Steel and absorbs 100% of damage dealt
In Sickness 80 instant (possibly circle AoE?); inflicts disease (20s)
Black Honeymoon 80 roomwide AoE; adds 950 damage (Steel-piercing) for each player who was hit by In Health, or 9500 total damage at 5 In Health hits
Cold Feet n/a 360 degree gaze AoE; inflicts confused (10s)

Notes:

  • Rotation:
    • Until 90% health:
      • Dark Harvest
      • In Health (donut)
      • In Sickness
    • Uses Black Honeymoon once at 90% health
    • From 90% to 60% health:
      • Cold Feet
      • In Health (x2: pointblank, then donut)
      • Dark Harvest
      • In Sickness
    • Uses Black Honeymoon once at 60% health
    • Below 60% health:
      • Cold Feet
      • In Health (x2: pointblank > donut OR donut > pointblank)
      • Dark Harvest (x2)
      • In Health (donut) + adds
        • Summons untargetable adds outside the arena which will cast either a single circle AoE (potency 250) or four line AoEs (each potency 300), resolving shortly before In Health
        • Avoid the AoEs, then run toward Edda to dodge In Health
      • Black Honeymoon
  • Stay close to avoid In Health easily
    • But be careful of the disease debuff from In Sickness in the first half of the fight
  • Make sure to turn for Cold Feet
