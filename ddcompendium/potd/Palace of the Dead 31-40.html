PotD 31-40

< Prev
Next >

Nothing too exciting here, but there are lots of wall traps to watch out for.

Pomanders of resolution and raising do not drop at all on this set. Affluence, flight, and alteration do not drop on floor 39.

Potsherds do not drop from bronze chests on floors 31-33.
