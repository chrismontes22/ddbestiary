Deep Dungeon Floor Guide

A floor-by-floor companion for FFXIV Deep Dungeons

About This Guide

Navigating Deep Dungeons in Final Fantasy XIV can be overwhelming, especially when trying to remember which enemies have sight, sound, or proximity aggro on a specific floor.

This guide takes the enemy data from the Deep Dungeon Compendium and reorganizes it into a floor-by-floor format. By breaking the information down one floor at a time, you can easily pull this up on a second monitor or your phone while playing to quickly check enemy vulnerabilities, warnings, and aggro types without scrolling through massive tables.
